How Computers Help People With Disabilities

Cpmputers have become very powerful tools for those with disablities. It allows those who are less mobile to be able to perform more tasks independantly and can even help some who are blind to be able to see to an extent. In addition to this by adapting regular computers to be more accessible by disabled people, it allows people with health problems limiting how much they can go outside and recieve traditional education to be able to gain this education throguh omline tools.
